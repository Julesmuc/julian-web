What distinguishes me from other developers:

Agriculture

In my comprehensive studies of agricutural sciences and economics, I received a broad understanding of many agricultural and economic process chains, including the up- and downstream market. After several internships and project works, I received my Master of Science in Agro-Economis in Munich.
I later used that deep knowledge of agriculture to work at an agricultural IT company and successfully participated in many agile projects with topics like fertilization, cross compliance documentation, account settlement and geo referenced information systems.

Software development

For the frontend part, I feel very comfortable with React.js, OpenUI5, Web components and Flutter/Dart for mobile development.
